INTERVIEW INSIGHTS SUMMARY

ACADEMIC CONTEXT

Material needs depend on the degree program. Systems Engineering students require very few physical materials.
"Ingeniería de sistemas… se centra mucho más en computación"

Books are not part of the material ecosystem. Professors provide PDFs instead of physical books.
"Nunca me han pedido libros físicos"

PURCHASING BEHAVIOR

Purchases are sporadic and tied to specific moments (exams or specific assignments).
"Únicamente se piden en trabajos muy específicos"

Campus stores are convenient only outside peak hours.
"Cuando no era hora pico… fue bastante sencillo"

Students avoid campus stores for tech items due to high prices and limited variety.
"Llegando a comprarlas al triple o más"

PLATFORMS AND TRUST

Trust outweighs convenience. The user relies more on platform guarantees than on individuals.
"Me fío más en la plataforma que en la persona"

Unprotected marketplaces are perceived as risky (Facebook Marketplace, WhatsApp).
"La fiabilidad es menor"

Face-to-face payment increases trust when using informal channels.
"Contraentrega es más necesario"

RESALE AND SECOND-HAND

Materials are kept if future use is expected.
"Sé que a futuro me lo van a seguir pidiendo"

Second-hand items are avoided due to reliability concerns, except when refurbished.
"Prefiero la fiabilidad de algo nuevo"
"Elementos reacondicionados"

OPPORTUNITY FOR A UNIVERSITY MARKETPLACE

Immediate availability is the main perceived value.
"Evitar tener que esperar el envío"

Adoption would be low for tech-focused students but higher for material-heavy programs.
"No creo que la usaría tanto"
